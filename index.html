Circuit Looper

  1. Enter a Workout Video URL from Youtube.
    Here's a good one to start: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ATfgamzs_vY
  2. Enter the start time of the workout. In the example about the workout starts at 1:28
  3. Enter the end time of the workout. In the example about the workout ends at 3:57
  4. Enter the amount of recovery time between each circuit. The default is 10 seconds.
  5. Choose whether or not to mute the workout video. This is nice if you want to play your own music.
  6. Click "Start Workout"

The workout will loop indefinitely. Enjoy!
